Logo Misuse

To maintain brand integrity, never alter the logo in any of the following ways. Consistency builds recognition and trust. These examples show what NOT to do.

Additional Don'ts
  • Don't place the logo on busy or low-contrast backgrounds
  • Don't add outlines, borders, or strokes to the logo
  • Don't use the logo as a pattern or texture
  • Don't animate the logo without approval
  • Don't crop or partially obscure the logo
  • Don't recreate the logo using different fonts
  • Don't combine the logo with other graphic elements
