Video Guidelines

Video is one of the most engaging ways to tell your brand story. Consistent motion principles ensure all video content feels cohesive, from social clips to full productions.

Video Specifications

1080p

TikTok 9:16 (1080×1920)
LinkedIn 16:9 (1920×1080) or 1:1 (1080×1080)
Motion Principles

Easing

Use ease-out for entrances, ease-in for exits. Never use linear motion.

Duration

300-500ms for UI elements. 800-1200ms for larger transitions.

Logo Animation

Logo should animate in smoothly, never pop. Hold for 2+ seconds at end.

Text Animation

Text fades or slides in. Never use cheesy effects like spinning or bouncing.
