Co-branding Guidelines

When partnering with other brands, maintain your brand integrity while respecting theirs. Clear co-branding rules prevent visual conflicts and ensure both brands are represented appropriately.

Rules
  • Our logo should be equal size or larger than partner logos
  • Maintain minimum clear space between logos (equal to logo height)
  • Use a divider line (1px grey) or "in partnership with" text
  • Never alter either logo's colours or proportions
  • Both logos should be in their full-colour versions when possible
  • On coloured backgrounds, both logos may use mono versions
  • Partner logo should never appear without our logo present
Approval Process

All co-branded materials require approval from the Brand team before production. Submit designs at least 5 working days before deadline. Include: high-resolution partner logo, context of use, and intended channels.
