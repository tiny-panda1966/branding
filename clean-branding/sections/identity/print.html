Print Specifications

Print production requires precise technical specifications. Following these guidelines ensures consistent colour reproduction and professional-quality output.

Production Specs

Bleed & Margins

Bleed 3mm all sides
Safety Margin 5mm from trim
Resolution 300 DPI minimum

Paper Stock

Business Cards 400gsm Silk
Brochures 170gsm Silk
Letterhead 120gsm Uncoated
File Requirements
  • Format: PDF/X-1a:2001 for offset, PDF/X-4 for digital
  • Fonts: Embedded or converted to outlines
  • Images: CMYK, 300 DPI, no compression
  • Black text: 100% K only (not rich black)
  • Rich black (large areas): C40 M30 Y30 K100
  • Spot colours: Defined as Pantone swatches
