Photography Guidelines

✓ Do

  • Use natural, soft lighting
  • Show real people, not stock models
  • Capture genuine emotions
  • Include diverse representation
  • Leave space for text overlays

✗ Don't

  • Use harsh flash photography
  • Choose overly staged images
  • Apply heavy filters
  • Use low-resolution images
  • Include dated technology
